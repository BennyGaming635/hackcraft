Build & Ship: Password Manager

Hackcraft is the YSWS track where teams and individuals build a password manager — simple, secure, and student-friendly. There’s an accompanying WS (Workshop/Side Quest) to integrate a hardware security key (like a YubiKey) for second-factor or resident secret storage.

Project constraints

  • Project must be primarily a password manager (desktop, mobile, or web).
  • Include a public repo link (GitHub/GitLab) or upload in the submission form.
  • Limit contributors to 5 people.
  • No paid third-party APIs for core functionality (OK for optional features).
  • Prefer permissive open-source license (MIT/Apache) and include a LICENSE file.
  • Do not include real passwords in your demo or repo.
  • Bonus: YubiKey support or a plan for hardware-backed secrets earns bonus points.

Questions? Reach out to the YSWS organisers or open an issue in the Hackcraft repo.
